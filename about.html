About Ethereality and Me:

Hello and welcome to Ethereality Designs! Here I believe that if you can dream it, you can do it.

My name is Autumn and I am just your average girl. I started Ethereality Designs with the hopes that I could help people achieve their dreams. Whether you are a small business owner, or starting out with creating your own thing! No matter what it is I am here for you.
A little about me:

I am a thirty year old, married, mom of two. I am currently halfway through a computer science degree and I love every second of it. When I have free time I often design, write or bake. Although somedays, I do get lucky and I can play my favorite games. Stardew Valley and Animal Crossing definitely my top two right now!
I have been designing graphics and coding websites since I was fourteen years old. (hello neopets!) It's always been a fun way to express myself.
I always feel that a design can be improved upon in one way or another. With that said, I will work tirelessly to make something that you've always dreamed of. Lets work together to create something beautiful.
